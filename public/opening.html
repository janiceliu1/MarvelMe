Welcome to Marvel Me

Welcome to Marvel Me, where you can be your favorite superhero! Play with a friend, prepare your energy, and fight!

Instructions:

1. Put on your pebble watch.

2. There are three moves: jerk up, jerk down, jerk forward.

3. Each move represents FIGHT, DEFEND, or TRICK. The rules are simple: TRICK defeats DEFEND, DEfEND defeats FIGHT, and FIGHT defeats TRICK

4. There will be three rounds. Whoever wins, is the next Marvel Superhero!
